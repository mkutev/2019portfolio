Security

If you wish to work from a different location than your original place of work, you must present your PSPC pass to the commissioner at the front, or let them know you are a PSPC employee. They will then run a check on you, and give you a temporary pass; this pass will enable you to open the front security doors, however you will need someone to give you access to the desired floor.

A solution to facilitate this process is under works, and should soon be in place to allow employees to freely move on and off the unassigned floors.

Lost and Found Services

Security sweeps are done each and every night, and lost items will be left with the lost and found services of each building. To find your missing items, please present yourself to the commissioner at the front of the building and they will be able to give you more information on the lost and found services.
