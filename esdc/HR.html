Human Resources

This tab will help you find the websites related to your pay information, training, and professional development for your time here at ESDC!

MyKey

MyKey is a compensation web application where you can find electronic copies of your paychecks, T4s, as well as other pay-related information

  • Get your MyKey

Compensation Web Applications

Compensation Web Applications is a suite of applications to help facilitate the paycheque process..

  • To register for a CWA account, you need a MyKey!

PeopleSoft

PeopleSoft is an HR application that you can use to request time off or leave to be approved by your manager.

  • To register for a PeopleSoft account, you will need to fill out a NSD ticket!

Confidential Report

All new employees are required to make a confidential report outlining potential conflicts of interests such as political activities, assets, and preferential treatment.

  • To make a confidential report, go here.

Canada School of Public Service / GCcampus

Similar to SABA, GCCampus and the CSPS have a wide range of training opportunities. It is a collection of rich, interactive and open online resources, videos, courses, seminars and events for you to take part in!

  • Create a MyAccount for GCcampus.

SABA

SABA is a system that contains a variety of self-paced training, registration information for group training and information sessions, and webinars available from any device with an intranet connection.

To register for a SABA account, you will need to fill out a NSD ticket!

As a new employee, there are mandatory training courses that must be completed:

  • Orientation to ESDC
  • Security 101: It Starts With You!
  • Information Management: Yours to Manage!
  • IT Security Essentials
  • Stewardship of Information and Workplace Behaviours
