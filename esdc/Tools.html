Tools and Applications

GCpedia

Similar to Wikipedia, GCPedia can be used to create pages about a variety of projects, events, and other ideas.

  • Create a GCpedia account
  • FAQ
  • Learn to use GCpedia

GCconnex

Share knowledge and ideas, connect with colleagues, make posts, and join groups.

  • Create a GCconnex Account
  • GCconnex homepage

GCcollab

GCcollab is a collaborative tool used for networking and joining conversations. It is an platform open to academics, students, and public servants.

  • Register for an account
  • Log In

GCintranet

The GCintranet is an internal web presence just for public servants. In one shared place you can find:

  • common high-level content, like pay information or messages from the Clerk;
  • information on cross-government campaigns and initiatives; and,
  • what your colleagues in other departments are working on.

National Service Desk (NSD)

This online tool allows you to report IT problems, get access to new software and applications, or request help to set up your phone or computer. As well, there are many self-help guides for many of the IT related issues.

  • Online NSD
  • 1-800-268-0408
