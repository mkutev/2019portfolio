Crime in Vancouver, Visualized

The following radar visualization is created using D3.js as a final project for IAT355. This visualization is intended to explore possible seasonal trends in crimes committed within the Vancouver area annually. The data used is provided by the Vancouver Police Department and can be found in the Open Data Catalogue from the City of Vancouver's website. The eight different types of crimes listed below are compared between 2010 up to and including 2015 and the chart for each year is listed from earliest to latest below. You can hover over the different shapes within the chart as well as over the individual points for each month. In the future, I would like to add much more interaction by combining all the charts into one visualization with a selectable legend that allows for easier comparison between the years as well as customizable charts with selectable data to be featured.
